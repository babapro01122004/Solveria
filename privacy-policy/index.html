Scope of Policy: Consumer Platform & Enterprise Infrastructure

Solveria operates a dual-tier architecture to serve different needs within the legal and financial technology sectors:

  • Consumer Platform: Everyday users utilizing our free, client-side estimation tools directly on solveria.org.
  • Enterprise Infrastructure: Professional law firms, agencies, and financial networks licensing our proprietary frontend calculation engines (iframes/widgets) for deployment on their own domains.

Data Sovereignty Statement

At Solveria, we adhere to a Zero-Knowledge Architecture. Unlike traditional financial or legal tools that upload your data to a central cloud, Solveria acts as a "Client-Side Only" platform. This means that the sensitive inputs you type into our calculators are processed exclusively in your browser's volatile memory (RAM) and are technically incapable of being intercepted, read, or stored by our servers.

When you use Solveria, your data remains on your device. We do not have a database of your income, debts, or case details because we designed the system to function without one.

Treatment of Sensitive User Inputs

Our tools are designed to handle highly sensitive personal information. To ensure your safety, we strictly limit the scope of data processing to your local device. This policy applies to two distinct categories of inputs:

  • Financial Inputs: Information regarding your income, debts, credit score estimations, mortgage details, and assets entered into our financial calculators.
  • Legal & Case Inputs: Details regarding incident dates, settlement demands, injury specifics, medical costs, and liability scenarios entered into our legal settlement tools.

The Promise: None of this data is transmitted to Solveria. If you close your browser tab, this data is instantly wiped from memory unless you have enabled local persistence (see "Browser-Based Persistence" below).

Enterprise Iframe & Hosted Widget Deployments (B2B)

For law firms and enterprise clients utilizing our $499 National AIO Licenses or custom iframe deployments on their own domains, our Zero-Knowledge Architecture strictly applies.

Solveria acts solely as the mathematical logic core. We do not inject tracking pixels, keyloggers, or data-harvesting scripts into our B2B deployed widgets. When a client interacts with a Solveria calculator hosted on a licensed partner’s website, the data processing remains entirely client-side. Any lead-capture forms integrated into the licensed tool route the data directly to the purchasing law firm. Solveria does not intercept, view, or store our B2B partners' proprietary lead flow.

Browser-Based Persistence

To improve your user experience, some of our calculators may utilize standard web technologies such as localStorage or sessionStorage. This allows your browser to "remember" your inputs if you accidentally refresh the page or return to the calculator later.

It is important to understand that this data resides solely in your specific browser cache, not on our servers. You maintain full control over this data. You can wipe it at any time by clearing your browser's cache or cookies.

Aggregate Telemetry vs. Identity

We utilize third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect Aggregate Telemetry. This data helps us understand how our site is performing regionally, but it is strictly separated from your calculator inputs.

For example, we may track that "500 users visited from California" to ensure our California-specific legal modules are loading correctly. However, we cannot link this aggregate data to your specific financial situation or legal case details. We track the health of the application, not the identity of the user.

Legal Network Routing & Pay-Per-Call Disclosures

Solveria provides free, browser-based mathematical estimations. We operate on a transparency-first business model.

Solveria does not collect, store, or sell consumer contact forms, names, or email addresses. If a user wishes to act on their estimate, they may utilize our direct-call routing features to connect with certified third-party legal networks.

In this capacity, Solveria acts purely as a telephony bridge and routing service. We may receive affiliate compensation for successful connections made through our platform. Once you are routed to a third-party legal network, your interaction is governed by their separate privacy protocols.

Enterprise Software Purchasing & B2B Licensing

For professional firms purchasing licenses for our B2B infrastructure, payment processing is handled entirely by our encrypted, PCI-compliant Merchant of Record (Lemon Squeezy). Solveria does not process, view, or store your raw credit card data or financial routing information.

We retain only the minimal billing telemetry and contact data (such as the Firm's designated technical contact email and deployment URL) strictly necessary to issue your software license, generate your custom iframe, and provide ongoing infrastructure support.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Solveria.org does not sell your personal financial, legal, or case data to any third party. As stated above, the sensitive data you enter into our calculators is processed locally and is never stored on our servers.

Furthermore, we do not utilize third-party targeted advertising networks (such as Google Ads) that track your browsing behavior across the internet.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our technology or legal obligations. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
