Frequently Asked Questions

Are Solveria's calculators really free to use?+

Yes, absolutely. All of our calculators and tools are 100% free to use. We believe everyone should have access to high-quality financial tools. The site is supported by advertising, which allows us to keep our resources free for everyone.

How do you ensure the accuracy of your calculations?+

Our calculators are built on standard, industry-accepted financial and mathematical formulas. We rigorously test each tool to ensure accuracy. For financial tools like tax or mortgage calculators, we use the latest available data for our default values (e.g., federal tax brackets).

Do I need to create an account to use the calculators?+

No, you do not need to create an account. All of our tools are accessible to everyone without any sign-up required. You can start calculating immediately.

Is my financial data safe?+

Yes. We prioritize your privacy. All calculations happen directly in your web browser. We do not see, track, or store any of the personal financial numbers you enter into our calculators.
