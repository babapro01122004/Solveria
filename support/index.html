Statutory Compliance & Labor Logic

Solveria is engineered to be intuitive, but robust employment analysis requires context. Below you will find documentation on our calculation methodologies, data sources, and troubleshooting protocols.

Transparency & Methodology

Our Decision Engine utilizes state-specific labor codes and FLSA (Fair Labor Standards Act) federal baselines. While we strive for 100% accuracy, results should be viewed as high-fidelity statutory estimates rather than binding legal counsel.

Data Source Logic

  • State-Specific Labor Codes: Algorithms account for local minimum wage variances, overtime thresholds, and state-specific premium penalties (e.g., California DLSE meal break rules).
  • Federal FLSA Baselines: National-level tools are indexed against the most current Fair Labor Standards Act guidelines for baseline compliance.
  • Enterprise Deployment: For B2B Licensees: All our frontend applications are built on vanilla HTML5/JS for conflict-free integration into your firm's existing CMS or web architecture.

Technical Troubleshooting

Because Solveria operates on a Client-Side Architecture, performance relies on your browser's capabilities. This ensures your data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers for calculation.

  • Calculators not loading? Ensure JavaScript is enabled. We recommend disabling aggressive ad-blockers that may interfere with our visualization scripts (Chart.js or D3.js libraries).
  • Data not saving? We use LocalStorage to persist your inputs for convenience. Clearing your browser cache or using Incognito Mode will reset your scenarios.
  • Licensee Integration: Firms who purchased a tool via the Lemon Squeezy portal should refer to the included README.md file for CSS customization and iframe deployment guidelines before contacting support.

Statutory Updates & Edge Cases

Labor laws evolve rapidly. If your firm notices a recent amendment to state labor codes or a municipal wage ordinance that conflicts with our logic, please file a report via the Contact page. We prioritize statutory updates within 48 hours.
