Contact & Support Operations

Solveria is a mathematical software provider, not a law firm. We cannot evaluate personal wage claims, provide legal counsel, or answer legal questions via email. Consumers seeking legal assistance should utilize the direct-call features within our free estimation tools.

To ensure the most accurate response, please categorize your inquiry. Solveria’s support team monitors channels Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM EST.

  1. Technical Support & Bug Reports: For edge-case anomalies or UX issues within our Employment Law & Wage decision engines. Please include the specific tool name (e.g., 'CA Unpaid Wages Application' or 'AIO Wage Engine') in your subject line. Priority support is reserved for verified B2B Enterprise Licensees.
  2. Strategic Partnerships & Media: For affiliate integration proposals, press inquiries, or syndication rights. We prioritize partnerships that align with our privacy-first architecture.
  3. Legal & Compliance: For inquiries regarding our Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, or Data Sovereignty protocols.

All inquiries are currently routed through our central dispatch:

contact@solveria.org

Please allow up to 48 hours for a response as we prioritize technical stability and data accuracy.
